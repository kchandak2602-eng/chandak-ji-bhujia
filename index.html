OUR STORY

Four Generations of Sweet Perfection

Born in the heart of Bikaner, Rajasthan, Chandak Ji was founded on the promise of uncompromising purity. We use water from regional aquifers, pure buffalo ghee churned at dawn, and local spices ground by hand on traditional stone mills (Sil-Batta).

Every batch of our legendary Rasgulla and spicy Bikaneri Bhujia is overseen by master halwais, maintaining the precise temperatures and timings passed down through generations.
